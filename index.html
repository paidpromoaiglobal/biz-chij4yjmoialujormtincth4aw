About Us

THE GOAT HEALTH & FITNESS UNISEX GYM

THE GOAT HEALTH & FITNESS UNISEX GYM is Nazarathpettai's top-rated fitness centre, proudly serving the community at Thirupur Kumaran Street with a perfect 5-star reputation backed by over 119 happy members. We offer a welcoming, clean, and fully equipped training environment designed for everyone — from absolute beginners to seasoned athletes — with dedicated coaches who guide you every step of the way with proper techniques, motivation, and personalised nutrition advice.

At THE GOAT GYM, we believe fitness is a family affair. Our knowledgeable and friendly trainers treat every member like their own, creating a supportive atmosphere where women feel safe and comfortable, outstation newcomers feel at home, and every individual achieves their personal best. Open six days a week from 5:30 AM to 10:00 PM and Sundays for morning sessions, we make it easy to fit world-class training into your daily life.
